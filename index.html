About Us

Dice Ventures is a leading manufacturing company based in Kwale town, Ndokwa West, Delta State. We specialize in providing comprehensive manufacturing solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of Nigerian industries. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern equipment with skilled craftsmanship to deliver products that meet international quality standards. With deep roots in the Delta region, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities of manufacturing in Nigeria, and we're committed to contributing to local industrial development while serving clients nationwide.
